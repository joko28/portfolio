Artist Statement


I work in digital art and photography where I bring out different perspectives of life. My favorite genre is fantasy because, in a world like ours, taking myself away from reality is a deal-breaker once in a while. I like to imagine myself in a different world where I can breathe with creativity and passion. The audience that I want to introduce my artwork to is anyone as I try to bring my imagination to life through art. There is a variety of mediums that I have used which you can see in my portfolio, I mainly want to focus on photography, Photoshop, Illustrator, Blender, and video editing. Taking certain things from reality and transforming them into something new, different, and compelling will reintroduce the audience to a meaningful experience.
