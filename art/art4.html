Art 24: Beginning Drawing | Contour & Cross-Contour Lines

For this assignment, I got to understand & show the volume of objects and figure out the differences between mechanical and modulated lines when seen or drawn. We had to pick out an area with many objects for the first contour and choose specific objects for the cross-contour line drawings.
