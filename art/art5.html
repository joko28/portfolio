Art 24: Beginning Drawing | Value

For this assignment, I had to use two-three eggs, position them together placed on a plate right on top of a table/counter with direct artficial light from the ceiling above at a certain angle. This allowed me to draw out the shadows under the eggs, giving it a realistic still life.
