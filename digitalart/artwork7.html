Minecraft & 3D Print

2019, 3D Print

This was my survival base, it was only my basement room, to get away from all the monsters, but I also had created a fort above this that has two levels. Unfortunately I could not take a photo of it because I no longer have access to the account.

I chose to make a camera for this assignment as my cultural significance because I love photography. To me photos are my memories, I don’t have a great memory like other people, and looking at photos helps me remember. They are my memories and my past. I enjoy looking at photos and what goes on within them because they all have their own special messages and different feelings that come with them. I also love holding a camera, it makes me feel professional and that I have a purpose. I have been doing photography since my 8th-grade yearbook. Photography has become my hope and dream, even though I still need to work on taking photos I am trying to get better and hopefully make a career out of it.
