Puffball

2022, P5.js, 2022

This is my little digital avatar, titled Puffball. I chose puffball because it was a cute name for my character. My avatar represents me in a way because I like cute little things, pastel colors, visual symmetry…basically a little bit of my personality. It has a spiky tail and horns to symbolize demon characteristics, my inner demon. An evil character that is actually nice and adorable.

Features: Tail moves with mouse hovering over, Background changes color with left & right keys, Mic input to the background circle framing, Cubes in background moves w/ x & y-axis, plus changes color with frameRate
