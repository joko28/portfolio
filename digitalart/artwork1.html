Galaxy Garden

2020, 3D Model

To show the beauty of the stars, because nowadays no one cares about nature as much as they used to. People are always on their phones, computers, or any type of technology and not being grateful for what nature has given us. The bunny represents peace and love because the bunnies are innocent and adorable. The elephant represents possibilities and adventure because they are only found in special areas of the planet, you can't see them near unless you go to a zoo. The tree represents life and death because it is green, grows, feeds, and can also die like a human. Nature is everything in this world, and everyone takes that for granted, so this garden represents what people have been missing.
